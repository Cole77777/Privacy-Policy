Privacy Policy – Dribbling Madness

Last updated: February 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile game Dribbling Madness (“the App”) handles information when you use the App on your device.

1. Who we are

Dribbling Madness is a basketball-themed idle/clicker game. The App is offered by the developer(s) of Dribbling Madness (“we”, “us”).

2. No account required

You can play Dribbling Madness without creating an account or providing any personal information such as your name, email, or phone number.

3. Information we do not collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or other device data
  • Any analytics or usage data that identifies you

4. Data stored on your device

The App may save game progress (e.g., points, upgrades, achievements) locally on your device so your progress is kept between sessions. This data stays on your device and is not sent to our servers.

5. Device features

The App may use:

  • Sound – optional in-game sounds
  • Haptics – optional vibration feedback (where supported)

These do not involve collection of personal data.

6. Third-party services

The App is built with common mobile frameworks (e.g., Expo/React Native). If we use any third-party services in the future that process data, we will update this policy and describe them here.

7. Children

Dribbling Madness does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App is intended for general audiences.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will be revised when we do. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the App, contact us at colemilstein2011@gmail.com, or use our Support page.
